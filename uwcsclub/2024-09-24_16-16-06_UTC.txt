📢 Join the Projects Program: Bring Your Ideas to Life! 

Get the chance to learn and build a passion project with an awesome team over the course of the Fall term! UW CSC is excited to support you by providing structure and guidance through mentorship, co-working sessions (with food!), and finally presenting your work for a chance to win a prize. 🚀

Applications for team leads and team members have officially opened. You can sign up as a group or as individuals (where we’ll match you with a team). As a team member, you’re responsible for creating a project, collaborating with your group, and learning along the way. No prior experience required! As a team lead, you’ll additionally be responsible with leading a group and ensuring everyone stays on track. ✨
